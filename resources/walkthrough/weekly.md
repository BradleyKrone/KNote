# Weekly notes & quick capture

`Ctrl+Alt+W` opens this week's note, creating it from your weekly template
(with `{{date}}`, `{{title}}`, and `{{weekdays}}` expanded).

`Ctrl+Alt+J` quick-captures a timestamped bullet into this week's note from
anywhere — no need to switch files.

Configure the weekly folder, filename format, and template in
**KNote: Open Vault Settings**.
