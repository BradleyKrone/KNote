# Open the Kanban board

Press `Ctrl+Alt+K` (or run **KNote: Open Kanban Board**).

Every checkbox task in your vault appears as a card — `- [ ]` in To Do,
`- [x]` in Done, and any custom status chars you configure in between.

Drag a card to another column and KNote rewrites exactly one line in the
source note. Edit the checkbox in the note and the card moves. Filter by
text, tag, Status Changed, Date Entered, or Due date.
